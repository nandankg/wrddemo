PPMS – Project Progress Monitoring System

Component-A

Objective

A centralized platform for monitoring all WRD projects, fund requisitions, physical progress, financial progress, GIS mapping, dashboards, and management reporting.

Scope of Work
Redevelopment and modernization of existing PPMS.
Web and mobile application.
Fund requisition workflow.
Project monitoring and milestone tracking.
Physical and financial progress monitoring.
GIS / Google Maps integration.
SMS and OTP integration.
MIS and BI dashboards.
Multi-level approval workflows.
Real-time analytics and reporting.
Security audit and hosting.
Training and AMC support.
Major Modules
Module A – Fund Requisition
Fund request creation
Escalation workflow
Finance review
Sanction approval
Fund release tracking
Module B – Reporting Management
Custom reports
PDF/Word/Excel exports
Scheduled reports
Monthly MIS generation
Project-wise and division-wise reports
Module C – MIS Dashboard
Real-time monitoring
KPI tracking
Progress tracking
Pendency analysis
Module D – BI Dashboard
Interactive charts
GIS visualization
Drill-down analytics
Financial and performance metrics
